What the NAR Settlement Means for You

In 2024, a landmark settlement changed how buyer's agent fees work in the US. The short version: buyer's agent compensation is now negotiated separately — it's no longer bundled invisibly into the sale price. This is good for buyers. It means you have the right to know what your agent charges before you commit. The catch: most platforms still don't show it. GOZ shows it on every listing card — before you click, before you call, before you're emotionally invested in a house.
