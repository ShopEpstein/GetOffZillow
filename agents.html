Common Questions

We Heard You.
Here's the Truth.

Is this 3% of the sale price or my commission?

Your commission. On a $400K sale at 2.5%, your gross is $10,000. We take $300. Every platform in this space charges a percentage of commission — most charge $2,500–$4,000 on the same deal.

How do buyers actually find my listings?

Buyers search GOZ directly by city, ZIP, or address. Listings sort by recency — not ad spend. As more agents join, more buyers come. You can also share your direct listing URL anywhere you already market.

What if I already use Zillow or Agent Pronto?

GOZ is an additional channel — not a replacement. List the same properties here free. If a GOZ buyer closes, you pay $300 instead of $3,500. No risk running both. A few things worth knowing: In 2025, the FTC sued Zillow and Redfin for allegedly coordinating to suppress competition in rental advertising. A class-action (Hagens Berman, 2024) alleges Premier Agent steers buyers toward Zillow-affiliated lenders. And Zillow removed wildfire and flood risk data after sellers complained it hurt home values. These aren't reasons to panic — they're reasons to diversify.

How does GOZ make money if listing is free?

3% of your commission only when a GOZ buyer closes with you. No subscriptions, no advertising, no data sales. Our incentive is aligned with yours — we only earn when you earn.

Is GOZ available in my state?

Yes — any licensed agent in any state can list free. We started in Florida and are now open nationwide. Listings go live immediately in search wherever you are.

What's the catch?

There isn't one. We charge less because we operate with less overhead — no call centers, no lead teams, no manual matching. You list yourself, buyers find you directly. We take a small cut when it works. If it never works, you pay nothing.
