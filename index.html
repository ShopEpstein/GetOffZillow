Transparency

How We Make Money

You should know exactly how this works before you sign up. No surprises.

🤝

Only When You Close

We earn 3% of your gross commission when a transaction closes with a buyer who contacted you through GOZ. If no deal closes, we earn nothing. No close = no charge.

🚫

Never Upfront

No monthly fees. No listing fees. No per-lead charges. Agents pay nothing to join, list, or receive buyer inquiries. Your first dollar to us is your $300 on a successful close.

🔒

No Data Sales

We don't sell buyer or agent data to advertisers, lenders, or data brokers. Your contact info is shown to buyers for the listing — that's the product. That's all.

📊

Commission Is Public

Post-NAR settlement, buyer's agent compensation must be negotiated separately. We display it publicly on every listing by design — not just for compliance, but because buyers deserve to know.

⚖️

Honor System + Accountability

Closings are reported by agents through the dashboard. We operate on professional trust. Agents who circumvent the fee agreement have their accounts closed.

🌎

Starting in the Gulf South

We're building from Pensacola out — the Floribama corridor first, then the broader Gulf Coast. Small enough to know our agents by name. That's intentional.
